Hi — I'm Pranjal, a PhD candidate in Economics at Georgetown University, advised by John Rust.

I work at the intersection of reinforcement learning and structural econometrics — using deep learning to study consumer demand, algorithmic pricing, and strategic behavior in markets. My recent work looks at consumer aesthetics in fast fashion, algorithmic collusion in auctions, and how large language models reason under uncertainty.
